EP 1000 Fablab Safety

Introduction to Fablab saftey

As the saying goes, 'Safety is our No. 1 priority.' This is a compulsory program for SP students to be trained and safety certified before allowing access and work in workshops, Fablabs or studio with fabricating facilities. The objectives are

  • To understand WSH regulationsin SP and the safepractices in Fablab workshops.
  • To familiarise with th safety infrastrucutre in Fablab workshops ansd to know machines' hazards.
  • To instill greater industry ownership of Occupational Safety & Health Standards.
  • To reduce risk at source by requiring all stakeholders to eliminate or minimize the risks they create at the workplace.
